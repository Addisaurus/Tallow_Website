{# FAQ Item 1 #}

What is beef tallow?

Beef tallow is rendered fat from cattle. Our tallow comes exclusively from 100% grass-fed beef, making it rich in vitamins and beneficial fatty acids that are incredibly nourishing for skin.

{# FAQ Item 2 #}

Will it clog my pores?

No! Beef tallow is non-comedogenic, meaning it won't clog pores. Because its composition is so similar to our skin's natural oils (sebum), it's easily absorbed and compatible with all skin types.

{# FAQ Item 3 #}

Does it have a smell?

Our tallow is highly refined and has minimal scent. We add a subtle blend of lavender and chamomile essential oils for a light, pleasant fragrance. It's not overpowering!

{# FAQ Item 4 #}

How long does shipping take?

Orders typically ship within 2-3 business days. Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.
