How to Use

1

Cleanse Your Skin

Start with clean, slightly damp skin for best absorption. Pat dry gently with a towel.

2

Apply a Small Amount

Take a pea-sized amount and warm it between your fingers. A little goes a long way!

3

Massage Gently

Massage into your skin using upward, circular motions. Focus on dry areas like elbows and knees.

4

Let It Absorb

Allow 1-2 minutes for full absorption. Use morning and night for best results.

Pro Tip: Store in a cool, dry place. If the product becomes too firm, warm a small amount in your hands before applying.
